About WENDI

WENDI, the Wellesley Evaluation Network for Dorm Information, is a collaborative web tool aimed atdemystifying the dorm selection process at Wellesley. Created in response to the annual chaos of room assignments, WENDI offers a streamlined and informative experience for students navigating their housing options.

Our Mission

The mission of WENDI is to simplify the housing selection journey for Wellesley students. By providing access to real-world data and peer reviews, WENDI ensures that no student feels left in the dark when choosing a dorm room. Whether you're looking for insights on various rooms or eager to share your own experiences, WENDI serves as your go-to platform.

The Team

In 2023 Fall, WENDI was brought to life by a dedicated team of students who experienced first-hand the challenges of dorm selection. The team includes Annabel Yao, Emma Lim, Hae Rin Hong, and Veronica Lin.
